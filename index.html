OVERVIEW

The With Ross Podcast began as an audio blog (aulog?) to share with my friends and family as I embarked on what I thought would be a multi-year trip around the world. Just three months into that trip, COVID put my plans on hold.But the podcast didn't stop.The podcast pivoted into a business show as I bought, ran, and eventually sold a doggy daycare.After that, I began talking more and more about technology and AI.The easiest way to describe my podcast is that it's a show at the intersection of:
- technology
- business
- and travel.I've had an absolute blast making it and it's something I plan to do for the rest of my life.
